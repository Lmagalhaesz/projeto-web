Our Story

From Enzo Ferrari's dream to becoming the most iconic automotive brand in the world, discover the milestones that shaped our legacy of excellence and innovation.

1947

The Beginning

Enzo Ferrari creates the first car to bear his name, the 125 S, marking the birth of a legend. The 12-cylinder engine becomes the heart of Ferrari DNA.

1950

Formula 1 Debut

Ferrari enters Formula 1, beginning an unparalleled racing legacy. The prancing horse becomes synonymous with championship glory and technological innovation.

1962

250 GTO Era

The legendary 250 GTO is born, combining racing pedigree with road-going capability. Today considered the most valuable classic car in the world.

1987

F40 Supercar

The F40 debuts as the ultimate expression of Ferrari's philosophy: raw performance, lightweight construction, and pure driving emotion.

2019

Hybrid Innovation

SF90 Stradale launches as Ferrari's first plug-in hybrid, proving that electrification can enhance rather than compromise performance.

2024

Future Forward

The F80 represents the pinnacle of hybrid technology, combining 1200 horsepower with advanced aerodynamics and sustainable innovation.
